VEGAN CHONDROITIN REPLACEMENT

Phytodroitin™

A 100% plant-origin, seaweed-derived alternative to animal chondroitin.

Chondroitin is widely used in bone and joint supplements but is traditionally sourced from shark, bovine, or porcine cartilage. Phytodroitin™ is a complex blend of specific, mucopolysaccharide-rich seaweed and algal extracts, carefully designed to mimic the exact structural and functional benefits of chondroitin. It offers a sustainable, ethical, and highly effective B2B solution.
