VEGAN COLLAGEN ALTERNATIVE

Vollagen®

100% plant-based complex of 18 amino acids in the exact ratio of human collagen.

Hydrolyzed animal collagen is traditionally sourced from bovine, marine, or porcine skins. Vollagen® is produced via a proprietary, multi-step fermentation and microencapsulation process starting from non-GMO corn. Because the amino acids are already isolated, they bypass the need for hydrolysis and are immediately bioavailable for skin, joint, nail, and hair health.
