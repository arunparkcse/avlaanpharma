Vitashine™ D3

The world's first FSSAI-approved, 100% lichen-derived Cholecalciferol (D3).

Conventional Vitamin D3 is derived from lanolin (extracted from sheep's wool). Vitashine™ D3 is a clean, sustainable, and 100% plant-origin alternative derived from wild-harvested Lichen. Highly stable and bioidentical to human D3, it provides the perfect clean-label solution for premium supplements.

Specifications & Formats

Vitashine™ D3 Oil

Oily liquid format, perfect for softgels, liquid drops, spray supplements, and food fortification. Highly stable in oil bases.

Solubility: Cold-water dispersible powder
