[01] OUR STORY & MISSION

Pioneering clean-label ingredients in India

We specialize in 100% plant-origin, clean-label alternatives to conventional ingredients, catering to the growing vegetarian, vegan, and sustainability-conscious markets in India and abroad.

For decades, standard formulations relied heavily on animal-derived products—gelatin for capsules, sheep lanolin for Vitamin D3, and animal cartilage for chondroitin. At Avlaan Pharma, we believe that clinical efficacy should not come at the cost of ethical principles or environmental sustainability.

By utilizing state-of-the-art bio-fermentation (such as Vollagen®) and sustainable lichen harvesting (such as Vitashine™ D3), we deliver active ingredients that perform identically to—or better than—their animal equivalents, with complete regulatory defensibility.
